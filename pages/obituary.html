In Memoriam

Dr. Steven Rose

President, Passaic County Community College

Dr. Steven Rose served as President of Passaic County Community College, dedicating his career to expanding access to education and supporting the students and faculty of PCCC. His leadership shaped the institution and left a lasting mark on everyone who had the privilege of knowing him.
