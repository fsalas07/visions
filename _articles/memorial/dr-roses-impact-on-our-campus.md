Dr. Steven Rose as always been very welcoming from the first day I started here. I remember him walking through our Financial Aid office more than a few times just to say hello and to meet us. He always made sure to speak to anyone down to the custodian and he took pride in showing respect and love to every person. He will be greatly missed as his presence was greatly felt in our college. Please know that he has touched many hearts and spirits and that I have grown here just because of his welcoming embrace every single time he spoke or had a conversation. He always wanted to see everyone evolving in life and he has always given the chance for great opportunities.
